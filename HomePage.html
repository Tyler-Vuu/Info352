Ethics and AI

In the rapidly evolving technology era, AI has become an integral part of our daily lives. From visually generative AI such as Midjourney to voice assistants like Siri and Alexa, AI has permeated various aspects of society. Moreover, AI now started to be used as crucial tools in public services, such as transportation and law enforcement. Race and AI

In this section, we aim to explore the complex and ongoing issue of AI and race. We will include examples that support the argument and highlight the challenges in our relationship with AI, particularly in how it mirrors, amplifies, and reinforces societal biases. Our goal is to encourage you to critically reflect on the role of AI in society and to offer a fresh perspective on the potential of AI to either perpetuate existing biases or prompt meaningful social change.

Gender and AI

In this section, we will delve into the issues that have become increasingly prominent in our society today. With the introduction of examples from both the past and today, we hope to call attention to the persistent challenges that women continue to face. Our objective is to foster a deeper understanding of these challenges, encouraging you to reflect on the progress made and the path forward in achieving gender equality.

Combating the Issue

We know that the challenges surrounding these issues are not easily resolved. However, we want to present some potential solutions that could contribute to the evolution of AI. Our intention is to start constructing ideas on how technologies can be harnessed to serve society better, and inspire readers to brainstorm the possible solutions.
