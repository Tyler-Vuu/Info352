Conclusion

There is no doubt that AI is and will be an extremely important tool for us to utilize even with its many flaws. This is why it is more important than ever to try to address the ethical problems surrounding AI while it is in the early stages and not to get caught up in the rabid advancement of machine learning technology. We have already started to see the effects of what an unethically trained AI can look like and how it can reinforce stereotypes of genders and minorities. But if we slow our approach towards AI and make sure that we build it with a strong ethical foundation, it can create a more equal society and improve the lives of all people instead of the top few.
